How OpenChat handles your files

OpenChat has no central storage. Photos, videos, and files you send are shared peer-to-peer and held on your own device while you're online — nothing is uploaded to a server.

  • A file stays viewable in the chat as long as at least one person who has it is online.
  • When you close the tab, the files you shared stop being available to others until you return.
  • Anyone who already viewed a file keeps their own copy — sharing can't be "un-sent".
